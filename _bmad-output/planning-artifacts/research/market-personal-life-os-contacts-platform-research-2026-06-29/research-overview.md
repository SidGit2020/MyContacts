# Research Overview

This report presents comprehensive market research for a **Personal Life OS — Intelligent Contacts & Relationship Management Platform** targeting the Indian market. Conducted in June 2026, it covers six market domains: Personal CRM, Spiritual Wellness, Social Community, Career Development, Financial Learning, and AI Personal Assistants — synthesising customer behavior, pain points, decision patterns, and competitive landscape data from 30+ verified web sources.

**Core Finding:** India's 1B+ internet users are served by dozens of single-purpose apps but no unified Life OS. The intersection of relationship management, spiritual community, and holistic personal growth represents the largest unoccupied whitespace in India's mobile app market. The Spiritual Community segment alone is a USD 261.2M India market by 2033, growing at 15.6% CAGR, with zero dominant community-oriented platform.
